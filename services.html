Full Detail — FAQs

How long does a full detail take?

Most vehicles take 3–5 hours depending on size and condition. We'll confirm a window when you book.

Is mobile service available for this?

Yes — full details are available by drop-off or mobile appointment across our Arizona service area.

How often should I get one?

Every 3–4 months for daily drivers keeps interiors and finishes in top condition between details.

Paint Correction — FAQs

Will correction remove all my swirl marks?

Most swirls and light scratches are removed. Deeper scratches may reduce significantly but not disappear entirely — we'll set expectations after inspection.

How many stages do I need?

We recommend a stage after inspecting your paint's condition — from a one-step gloss enhancement to full multi-stage correction.

Should I coat after correction?

Yes — we strongly recommend a ceramic coating immediately after correction to lock in and protect the finish.

Ceramic Coating — FAQs

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Gyeon® ceramic coatings are built for multi-year protection with proper maintenance — exact longevity depends on care and exposure.

Do I need paint correction first?

We recommend it — a coating locks in whatever condition the paint is in, so correcting defects first gives the best result.

Is there a community discount?

Yes — ask about our current thank-you offer: $100 off one coating, or $200 off each when you book two together.
